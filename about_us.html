What we are doing.

Publishing and, in particular, digital publishing has been massively disrupted by social networks, mobile, programmatic RTB which has resulted in a dramatic decline in advertising revenues. The latest research and trends show that audience increasingly expects news to come to them, through notifications and personalization. That’s Traqli’s core value proposition. We help digital publishers double revenue through increased audience engagement and loyalty.
